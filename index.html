RxV2

RxV2 embodies the idea that modern and accessible technologies, such as smartphones, should play an active role in health care and can lead to better patient outcomes.

Quit Smoking App

Quit Smoking is an innovative research proposal that looks at the effectiveness of using mobile applications to guide patients towards better health outcomes.

Would you like to help sponsor this project? Contact me.

Quit Smoking is a research project from RxV2 that is currently in the process of IRB approval at The Thomas J. Long School of Pharmacy and Health Sciences at the University of the Pacific.

The Quit Smoking app will be released on the US iOS app store Fall 2014.
