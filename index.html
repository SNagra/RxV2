Find OI on Facebook

Operation Immunization at UOP Thomas J. Long School of Pharmacy and Health Sciences is a committee that falls under APhA-ASP. Our goal is to promote public health through actively immunizing the public with Influenza, Tdap, and Pneumococcal vaccines. Operation Immunization is composed of first and second year student pharmacists at the Thomas J. Long School of Pharmacy and Health Science. Aside for our officers, we also utilize many student pharmacists from this school as well.

The Operation Immunization app will be released in the App Store in Sept. 2014.

Quit Smoking App

Quit Smoking is an innovative project and research proposal that looks at the effectiveness of using mobile applications to guide patients towards better health outcomes.

Would you like to help sponsor this project? Contact me.

Quit Smoking is a research project from RxV2 that is currently in the process of IRB approval at The Thomas J. Long School of Pharmacy and Health Sciences at the University of the Pacific.

The Quit Smoking app will be released on the US iOS app store Fall 2014.
